Pineapple Lemonade


A step by step Guide

How to make Pineapple Lemonade

Ingredients:

1/2 cup sugar

1 cup lemon juice

1 cup pineapple juice

2 tablespoons lime juice


Directions:

1.To make the simple syrup, combine sugar and 1 cup water in a medium saucepan over medium heat, stirring until the sugar has dissolved; let cool.

2.In a large pitcher, whisk together simple syrup, lemon juice, pineapple juice, lime juice and 5 cups water.

3.Place in the refrigerator until chilled.

4.Serve over ice.
